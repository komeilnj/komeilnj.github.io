Featured work

A proposed magnonic method for gravity gradient sensing

The manuscript develops a new sensing route in which a gravity gradient shifts a magnon resonance in a YIG cavity system. Operation near an exceptional point converts the very small shift into a larger spectral splitting that can be read through microwave measurements.
